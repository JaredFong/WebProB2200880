DIVE RIGHT INTO THE WARZONE

"Apex Legends" is a free-to-play battle royale game where legendary competitors from around the Frontier team up to battle for glory, fame, and fortune. Set in the Titanfall universe, players choose from a diverse cast of unique characters, each with their own abilities, to outwit and outfight opponents in fast-paced, squad-based combat.

WINNER WINNER CHICKEN DINNER

In a match of "Apex Legends," squads of three players drop onto an expansive map to scavenge for weapons, armor, and resources. The goal is to be the last team standing while a ring continuously shrinks, forcing players into increasingly intense encounters. Each player selects a unique Legend with special abilities, adding strategic depth to the fast-paced, tactical combat. The match ends when only one squad remains.
