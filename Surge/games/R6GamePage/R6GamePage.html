GADGETS IS YOUR GREATEST WEAPON

In a round of "Rainbow Six Siege" (R6 Siege), Attackers aim to secure an objective or eliminate all Defenders, while Defenders strive to prevent them. Players equip themselves with gadgets and weapons before the round starts, and it concludes when the objective is secured, time runs out, or one team eliminates the other. Each round typically lasts a few minutes.
